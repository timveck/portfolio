What I bring

Problem‑first, not content‑first

I don't start with slides. I ask: what's the actual performance gap? If training isn't the answer, I'll tell you. That comes from a decade of flexible problem‑solving across web dev, BI, and CRM – adapting to whatever the real need is.

Measure what matters

xAPI statements. LRS data. I don't guess what works – I watch learner behavior. Same approach I've used for years in BI and web dev: reviewing site performance, email open rates, and CRM data to refine and optimize.

AI as a design partner

I use DALL‑E, Leonardo, and Vidnoz for AI‑generated visuals and video content. Plus GPTs for scenario drafting and storyboard review. AI enhances the work – it never replaces my thinking.

Visuals + docs that scale

Photoshop, Canva, front‑end layouts – plus code comments, API specs, and rationale docs. You get clean designs and a full paper trail. Nothing is left to guesswork.

Translating experts into teachers

Client‑facing facilitation

I lead live 60‑minute demos, one‑on‑one coaching, group workshops, and mentorship for juniors and staff. I don’t just design - I share years of hard‑earned experience.
